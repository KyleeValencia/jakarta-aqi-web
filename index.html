    About this map

    A research forecast of morning air quality across Jakarta, predicted on a hexagonal H3 grid (resolution r7, ~1.4 km cells) covering mainland DKI Jakarta. A spatio-temporal graph neural network (ST-GCN) forecasts the Indonesian air-quality index (ISPU) at a pre-dawn anchor and the next 6 hours in 3-hour steps. Pick a location and the page resolves it to its hex cell and shows that cell's forecast.
